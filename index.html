Your Data Stays Private

All your exams, progress and scores are stored only on your device for complete privacy. Use ⬇ Copia de Seguridad to export your data and ⬆ Restaurar to import it on another device.

Once you have your exam file (in JSON format), click the 📂 Import Exam area to load it. You can also drag & drop files directly!

Practice & Learn

Use Practice Mode to test yourself with shuffled questions and track your scores. Review your mistakes with the Falladas filter to focus on what needs improvement.
